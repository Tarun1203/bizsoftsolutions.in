Every Tool a Food Business Needs

🪑

Table & Floor Management

Visual table layout with live status — vacant, occupied, billed. Merge/split tables, transfer KOTs, and handle multiple floors or sections (AC/non-AC/rooftop) with separate pricing if needed.

🖨️

Lightning KOT System

Order punched at the table prints instantly in the right kitchen station — tandoor, chinese, beverages each get their own KOT printer. Modifiers like "less spicy" travel with the order. Reprint, cancel and void with manager PIN control.

🛵

Zomato & Swiggy Integration

Online orders land directly in the POS — no separate tablets, no manual re-punching. Auto-accept rules, item availability sync, and one consolidated sales report across dine-in and delivery platforms.

🥘

Recipe-Level Inventory

Map ingredients to dishes; every paneer tikka sold deducts paneer, masala and oil automatically. Daily variance report shows theoretical vs actual consumption — catching pilferage and wastage where restaurants lose the most money.

📲

Captain App + KDS

Waiters take orders on a phone/tablet that fires straight to the kitchen display system. Kitchen marks dishes ready, captain gets notified — fewer trips, faster table turns.

💳

Fast Billing & Settlements

Split bills by item or amount, apply item/bill discounts with permission control, accept cash/card/UPI in one bill, and settle day-end with a single cashier report.

🎁

CRM & Loyalty

Customer database builds automatically from bills. Run points-based loyalty, birthday offers and SMS/WhatsApp campaigns to bring regulars back.

📊

Owner's Mobile App

Live sales, top items, hourly trends, void/discount alerts and day-end summaries on your phone — for one outlet or twenty, wherever you are.

🏪

Multi-Outlet Ready

Central menu and price control across branches, outlet-wise P&L, and inter-outlet stock transfer. Add outlets as you grow without changing systems.

FAQs

BillTouch POS — Common Questions

Will it work for my small café / cloud kitchen, or only big restaurants?
It scales both ways. A single-counter café uses just billing + KOT; a cloud kitchen uses online-order integration + recipe inventory; a fine-dine uses tables + captain app. You pay for the plan that matches your format.
Does it work if internet goes down during rush hour?
Yes — billing and KOT printing run locally and never stop. Online orders and cloud reports resume automatically when internet returns.
What hardware do I need?
Any Windows PC/laptop or Android device for billing, plus thermal printers (80mm recommended) for receipts and KOTs. We supply tested printer + cash drawer bundles and configure everything during installation.
Can it handle service charge, packing charge and GST on food correctly?
Yes — 5% GST on restaurant food, separate rates for beverages/MRP items, service charge with on/off per bill, packing & delivery charges for takeaway, all configured during setup.
How long does setup take?
Typically one day: installation, full menu entry, printer/KOT routing, tax setup and staff training. We stay on support during your first live service.
Can I see sales from home?
Yes — the owner's app shows live sales, item-wise reports, discounts/voids and day-end summary from anywhere. Multi-outlet owners see each branch separately and combined.
