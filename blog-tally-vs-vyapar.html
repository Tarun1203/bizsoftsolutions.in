Tally vs Vyapar: Which One Does Your Business Actually Need?

By BizSoft Solutions · Updated June 2026 · 6 min read

This is the most common question we get, and the answer is simpler than the internet makes it: Tally is accounting software that can bill. Vyapar is billing software that can account. Decide which job is primary in your business, and the choice makes itself.

Choose Vyapar if…

  • You're a retail/wholesale shop billing 20–300 invoices a day
  • The owner manages money, and the CA only needs monthly GST data
  • You want billing on mobile, payment reminders, and stock alerts
  • ₹18,000 + accountant dependency feels like overkill — because it is

See Vyapar features & live partner pricing.

Choose Tally if…

  • Your turnover/complexity demands audit-grade books (companies, audits, TDS/TCS, multiple godowns with job work)
  • A full-time accountant runs your data entry
  • Your CA insists on Tally data files, not exports

The hybrid answer most people miss

Plenty of growing businesses run both: Vyapar at the counter for fast billing, with monthly summaries entered into Tally for statutory books. And if you're already on Tally but hate being chained to the office PC, the fix isn't replacing Tally — it's adding Biz Analyst, which puts your Tally data, outstanding follow-ups and even voucher entry on your phone for about ₹3,000/user/year.

Don't buy Tally to feel professional, and don't force Vyapar to do an accountant's job. Match the tool to the job that happens 100 times a day in YOUR shop.
