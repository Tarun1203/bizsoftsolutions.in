What to Look for in GST Billing Software

Before comparing options, here's what matters most when choosing GST billing software for your Indian small business:

  • GST compliance — generates GSTR-1, GSTR-3B reports, handles CGST/SGST/IGST correctly
  • Ease of use — you shouldn't need an accountant to create a bill
  • Offline capability — critical in India where internet connectivity isn't always reliable
  • Mobile access — Android app so you can bill from anywhere
  • Inventory management — tracks stock automatically when you create bills
  • Pricing — affordable for small businesses with tight budgets
  • Support — responsive customer support in Hindi/regional languages
