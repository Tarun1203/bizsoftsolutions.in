Top Billing Software Options

1. Vyapar — Best for Retail & General Small Business

Vyapar is India's most popular billing app with over 1 crore downloads. The free plan is genuinely useful and covers GST billing, inventory tracking, and GSTR report generation. Paid plans start at just ₹1,499/year.

  • GST invoice in 30 seconds
  • Works offline on Android — no internet needed
  • GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B reports auto-generated
  • Thermal printer support for Bluetooth printers
  • WhatsApp invoice sharing built-in

Best for: Kirana stores, retail shops, pharmacies, service businesses, traders

2. Bill Touch — Best for Restaurants & Liquor Shops

If you run a restaurant, dhaba, cloud kitchen, or liquor shop, Bill Touch is purpose-built for you. Zomato and Swiggy orders flow directly into the POS, the kitchen display system reduces errors, and the liquor module handles excise compliance.

  • Zomato & Swiggy order integration
  • Table management with visual floor plan
  • Kitchen Display System (KDS)
  • Dedicated liquor shop billing module
  • Multi-outlet with consolidated reporting

Best for: Restaurants, hotels, cloud kitchens, liquor shops, food chains

3. Biz Analyst — Best for Tally Users with Field Sales

If your business already uses Tally and you have a field sales team, Biz Analyst is the missing piece. View your Tally data on your mobile, track sales staff with GPS, and let your team book orders from customer locations.

  • Tally data on your mobile — anytime, anywhere
  • GPS tracking for field sales executives
  • Mobile order booking synced back to Tally
  • Party outstanding and collection tracking
  • Works with Tally Prime and ERP 9

Best for: Distributors, wholesalers, FMCG, pharma distribution with field sales teams

Must-Have Features for Small Business Billing Software

  • GST-compliant invoices with GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes
  • GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B auto-report generation
  • Mobile app with offline capability
  • Inventory management with low-stock alerts
  • Payment reminders for customers
  • WhatsApp sharing of invoices
  • Simple enough to use without an accountant

Frequently Asked Questions

Which billing software is best for a small shop in India?

Vyapar is the best billing software for small shops in India. It has a free plan, works offline on Android, generates GST invoices instantly, and is trusted by over 1 crore businesses.

Is there any free billing software for small businesses in India?

Yes. Vyapar, My BillBook, and Zoho Books all have free plans. Vyapar's free plan is the most comprehensive — it includes GST billing, inventory, and GSTR report generation with no time limit.

What features should billing software for small business have?

The must-have features are: GST-compliant invoices, GSTR report generation, inventory tracking, mobile app with offline access, WhatsApp sharing of invoices, and payment reminder functionality.
