The question "Tally vs Vyapar — which is better?" is one of the most common questions we get from small business owners in India. The short answer: it depends on your business size and your needs. But for most small businesses, Vyapar wins on almost every practical dimension.

Let's break it down honestly.

Quick Overview

Tally has been the dominant accounting software in India for over 30 years. It's powerful, comprehensive, and almost every CA in India knows how to use it. However, it's expensive, complex, and requires significant training.

Vyapar launched in 2016 and quickly became India's most popular billing app by solving exactly the pain points Tally ignored: mobile access, offline use, simplicity, and affordability. It's not as powerful as Tally for complex accounting, but for small businesses it covers everything that matters.

Ease of Use — Vyapar Wins Easily

This is not even close. Vyapar was designed for business owners who are not accountants. You can create your first GST invoice within 5 minutes of downloading the app — no training needed.

Tally, on the other hand, has a steep learning curve. Even experienced users often rely on dedicated Tally operators or CAs to manage their books. If you're a business owner who wants to manage your own billing without an accountant, Tally is frustrating.

Pricing — Vyapar is Much More Affordable

This is one of the biggest differences between the two:

  • Vyapar: Free plan available. Paid plans start at approximately ₹1,499/year — less than ₹125/month.
  • Tally Prime: No free plan. Single user license costs approximately ₹18,000 upfront, plus annual maintenance charges (AMC) of around ₹4,500/year.

For a small kirana store or new startup, Vyapar's pricing makes far more sense. The total 3-year cost of Tally (₹18,000 + AMC) is about 10–15x higher than Vyapar for the same period.

Mobile App — Vyapar Wins

Tally does not have a proper mobile app. You can access it remotely via Tally on Cloud (paid add-on), but the experience is not designed for mobile.

Vyapar has a dedicated Android and iOS app that is smooth, fast, and fully functional. You can create bills, check stock, and see reports from anywhere on your phone — even without internet.

If you need to check your business from your phone while you're away from the office, Vyapar is clearly the better choice. (If you're on Tally and want mobile access, check out Biz Analyst — it gives you Tally data on your mobile.)

GST Compliance — Both Are Good

Both Tally and Vyapar are fully GST-compliant and generate GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and other required reports. Neither files directly on the GST portal — you or your CA will use the exported reports to file returns.

Vyapar's GST reports are easier to understand for non-accountants, while Tally's reports offer more granular detail that CAs prefer.

Final Verdict — Which Should You Choose?

✅ Choose Vyapar If You Are:

A small business owner (retail shop, trader, manufacturer, service provider) who wants simple, affordable, mobile-first billing and accounting without needing a full-time accountant. Budget-conscious. Need mobile access. Want to get started in minutes.

Choose Tally If You Are:

Running a medium or large business with complex accounting needs — multi-currency, payroll, complex manufacturing costs, consolidation of multiple entities. Have a dedicated CA or Tally operator. Can afford the higher cost. Don't need mobile access.

Our recommendation for 90% of small businesses in India: Start with Vyapar. It's free, easy to use, covers everything a small business needs, and you can always move to Tally later if your business scales to the point where you need it.
