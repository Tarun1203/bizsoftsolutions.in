Tally vs Vyapar 2026 — Which is Better for Indian Small Businesses?

By BizSoft Solutions  ·  Updated June 2026  ·  8 min read

Two of the most popular business software options for Indian small businesses are Tally Prime and Vyapar. Both handle GST billing, invoicing, and accounts — but they're built for very different types of users.

In this guide, we compare Tally vs Vyapar across pricing, features, ease of use, and GST support so you can pick the right one for your business.

Quick Verdict

Choose Vyapar if you're a small shop, retailer, or first-time software user who wants something simple and affordable. Choose Tally if you have a CA managing accounts, complex inventory needs, or run a medium-to-large business.

Pricing — Tally vs Vyapar

Vyapar has a genuinely free plan for mobile users. Paid plans start at ₹1,499/year (Silver Mobile) up to ₹3,599/year (Gold — mobile + desktop). For a small kirana or retail shop, this is very affordable.

Tally Prime starts at approximately ₹18,000/year for a Silver single-user license. The Gold multi-user license goes up to ₹54,000/year. This is a significant investment that makes more sense for businesses with dedicated accounting staff or a CA.

Ease of Use

Vyapar was built for non-accountants. You don't need to know accounting terms like debit/credit to use it. Create an invoice in 30 seconds, share on WhatsApp, done.

Tally has a steeper learning curve. It uses a keyboard-first interface designed around accounting concepts. Most small business owners end up needing a trained accountant or CA to use Tally effectively.

GST Compliance

Both Vyapar and Tally are fully GST-compliant. Vyapar automatically generates GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B reports which you can export and file on the GST portal. Tally has more advanced GST features including e-invoicing, e-way bills, and direct filing integration — which matters more for larger businesses with high invoice volumes.

Mobile App

Vyapar is mobile-first and works completely offline on Android. It's built around the mobile experience. Tally has added mobile features but its core experience is still desktop-first.

Who Should Choose Vyapar?

  • Small retail shops, kirana stores, pharmacies
  • Service businesses with simple invoicing needs
  • First-time software users who want to avoid complexity
  • Businesses with tight budgets — free plan is genuinely useful
  • Owners who want to manage everything from their phone

Who Should Choose Tally?

  • Businesses with a chartered accountant or accounting staff
  • Manufacturing businesses needing Bill of Materials
  • Companies with complex multi-location inventory
  • Businesses that need payroll management
  • Medium and large enterprises with high transaction volumes

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vyapar better than Tally for small shops?

For small shops, Vyapar is usually better than Tally because it is easier to use, has a free plan, works offline on mobile, and doesn't require an accountant. Tally is better for businesses with complex accounting needs.

Can Vyapar replace Tally?

Vyapar can replace Tally for basic GST billing, invoicing, and inventory. However, for advanced accounting, payroll, or manufacturing businesses, Tally Prime still has deeper features that Vyapar doesn't match.

What is the price of Tally Prime vs Vyapar?

Tally Prime starts at ₹18,000/year for a single user. Vyapar's paid plan starts at ₹1,499/year and has a free plan. Vyapar is significantly more affordable for small businesses.
