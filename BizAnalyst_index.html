No. Biz Analyst works with your existing Tally installation. You install a small connector on the PC where Tally is running, and your Tally data becomes accessible on your mobile app. No data migration needed.

Biz Analyst supports Tally ERP 9 and Tally Prime — the two most common versions used by Indian businesses.

Is my Tally data safe in Biz Analyst?

Yes. Biz Analyst uses end-to-end encryption. Your data is not stored on their servers permanently — it is fetched securely when you access the app.
