Common Questions
Biz Analyst FAQ
Everything you need to know before getting started
What is Biz Analyst?▼
Biz Analyst is a mobile app that connects to your Tally ERP and shows all your business data on your smartphone. It adds a sales team management layer on top of Tally — including GPS tracking, beat planning, order booking, and WhatsApp-based outstanding statements. It's used by 50,000+ businesses across India.
Does Biz Analyst work with Tally ERP 9?▼
Yes, Biz Analyst works with both Tally ERP 9 and Tally Prime. You need to install a small connector plugin on the PC where Tally is installed. Once set up, Biz Analyst syncs your data live whenever Tally is running on that machine.
Can I use Biz Analyst without Tally?▼
No — Biz Analyst requires Tally to be installed and running on your PC to sync data. It is a companion app for Tally, not a standalone billing or accounting solution. If you're looking for standalone software, we recommend Vyapar instead.
How does GPS tracking work in Biz Analyst?▼
Sales reps install Biz Analyst on their own phones. As they travel and visit parties, the app records their GPS location at regular intervals. As the owner or manager, you see each rep's live location on a map, their party visit history with timestamps, and daily movement reports. The rep cannot turn off location tracking without the manager knowing.

Does Biz Analyst work offline?▼
The Biz Analyst app requires internet to sync with your Tally server. The app caches recently viewed reports, so you can see them briefly offline, but live data requires the phone to be online and Tally to be running at the office.
Can sales reps book orders through Biz Analyst?▼
Yes! This is one of Biz Analyst's most popular features. Sales reps can create sales orders on their phone from the customer's location. These orders sync directly into Tally, where the office team can convert them into invoices. This eliminates phone calls and manual order entry, saving significant time.
How is Biz Analyst different from Credflow?▼
Biz Analyst focuses on sales team management — GPS tracking, order booking, beat plans, and Tally data on mobile. Credflow focuses on receivables collection — automated WhatsApp/SMS payment reminders and invoice discounting. Many businesses use both together: Biz Analyst for sales operations and Credflow for collections. They are complementary, not competing.
