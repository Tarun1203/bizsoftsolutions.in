Frequently Asked Questions

What is Biz Analyst app?

Biz Analyst is a mobile app that lets business owners view their Tally accounting data on their smartphone in real time. It also provides GPS-based sales team tracking, order booking, and party outstanding management.

What is the price of Biz Analyst app?

Biz Analyst pricing starts at ₹3,300 per year for the One Year Plan. The Business Plan is ₹6,600 (2 years) and the Five Years Plan is ₹9,900. All plans include unlimited users and full features.

Does Biz Analyst work with Tally Prime?

Yes, Biz Analyst works with both Tally Prime and Tally ERP 9. You need to install a small connector on the computer running Tally, and the data syncs to the Biz Analyst mobile app.

Can Biz Analyst track my sales team?

Yes, Biz Analyst has a built-in GPS tracking feature for field sales staff. Managers can see real-time location of all field executives, view their visit history, and monitor attendance.

Is Biz Analyst suitable for distributors and wholesalers?

Yes, Biz Analyst is especially popular among distributors and wholesalers. It allows field sales staff to book orders directly from their phones, which are then synced back to Tally automatically.
