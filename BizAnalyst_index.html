Frequently Asked Questions

What is Biz Analyst?

Biz Analyst is a mobile app that connects to your Tally ERP 9 or TallyPrime software and lets you view real-time business reports, sales data, outstanding payments, and more — directly on your smartphone.

What is the price of Biz Analyst?

Biz Analyst offers three plans: One Year at ₹3,300/year, Business Plan at ₹6,600 for 2 years, and Five Years at ₹9,900. All plans include full features and mobile access to Tally data.

Does Biz Analyst work with TallyPrime?

Yes, Biz Analyst works with both Tally ERP 9 and TallyPrime. You install a small connector on the PC running Tally, and the app syncs data to your phone in real time.

Can I use Biz Analyst without Tally?

No, Biz Analyst requires Tally ERP 9 or TallyPrime installed on a Windows PC. It acts as a companion app to bring Tally data to your mobile device.

What reports can I see in Biz Analyst?

Biz Analyst shows sales reports, purchase reports, party-wise outstanding, stock summary, profit & loss, balance sheet, cash & bank position, and much more — all synced from your Tally in real time.
