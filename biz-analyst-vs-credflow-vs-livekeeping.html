Biz Analyst vs Credflow vs LiveKeeping 2026 — Which is Best for Indian Businesses?

By BizSoft Solutions  ·  Updated June 2026  ·  8 min read

If you use Tally and want to manage your business on mobile, there are three popular options in India: Biz Analyst, Credflow, and LiveKeeping. They all aim to help you access accounts and manage cash flow on your phone — but they work very differently.

Quick Verdict — 2026

🥇 Biz Analyst — Best if you want Tally on mobile + GPS sales team tracking + order booking
🥈 Credflow — Best if your focus is receivables management and credit risk
🥉 LiveKeeping — Best for small businesses that want mobile bookkeeping without Tally

What Each App Does

Biz Analyst — Tally on Mobile + Sales Team Tracking

Biz Analyst connects directly to your Tally installation (Prime or ERP 9) and lets you view all your accounting data on your phone in real time. It's more than just a Tally viewer — it also tracks your field sales team via GPS, lets them book orders from customer locations, and manages party outstandings.

Requires Tally: Yes. Biz Analyst is a companion app — not standalone.

Credflow — Receivables & Cash Flow Management

Credflow specialises in helping businesses manage their receivables (money owed by customers) more efficiently. It connects with Tally, ERP systems, and accounting software to give you a credit risk view of your customer base, automated payment reminders, and cash flow forecasting.

Best for: Businesses with large receivables books and credit-based sales (B2B).

LiveKeeping — Mobile Bookkeeping without Tally

LiveKeeping is a standalone mobile accounting and bookkeeping app that doesn't require Tally. It's designed for small business owners who want to do basic accounts on their phone — record transactions, track expenses, manage cash, and view simple reports — without any prior accounting knowledge.

Requires Tally: No. Fully standalone.

Who Should Choose Each?

Choose Biz Analyst if…

  • You already use Tally and want to see your data on mobile
  • You have field sales executives and want to track them with GPS
  • You want field staff to book orders that auto-sync to Tally
  • You're a distributor, wholesaler, or FMCG company

Choose Credflow if…

  • Your biggest challenge is collecting outstanding payments
  • You have many B2B customers with credit terms
  • You want to assess credit risk before giving credit to customers
  • You need automated payment reminders and cash flow forecasting

Choose LiveKeeping if…

  • You don't use Tally and don't want to learn it
  • You want basic mobile bookkeeping for a small shop or service business
  • You're a first-time software user looking for simplicity
  • Budget is the primary concern — it's the cheapest option

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better — Biz Analyst or Credflow?

Biz Analyst is better if you want Tally on mobile, GPS sales team tracking, and order booking. Credflow is better if your primary need is receivables management, credit risk monitoring, and cash flow prediction. They serve different use cases.

Does Biz Analyst work without Tally?

No, Biz Analyst requires Tally (Prime or ERP 9) to be installed on a desktop. It connects to Tally via a small connector and displays the data on mobile. It cannot work as a standalone accounting system.

What is LiveKeeping used for?

LiveKeeping is a mobile-first bookkeeping and accounting app designed to let business owners do basic accounts on their phone without a CA. It is not a Tally companion like Biz Analyst, but a standalone mobile accounting solution.
