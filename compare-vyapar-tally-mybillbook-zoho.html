Verdicts by business type

Retail shop, kirana, garments, mobile store → Vyapar

Fast offline billing, barcode, stock alerts, UPI on invoice, and reports your CA accepts — at the lowest credible price. myBillBook is the closest competitor; between the two, compare renewal pricing and the local support you get. See live Vyapar partner pricing.

Audit-heavy, company structure, manufacturing → TallyPrime

Nothing matches Tally for statutory books, TDS/TCS, job work and CA workflows. Pair it with Biz Analyst for mobile dashboards and collections — that combination beats replacing Tally.

Service business, agency, online-first startup → Zoho Books

Bank feeds, automation, client portals and direct GST filing are genuinely excellent — if your team lives online and nobody bills at a physical counter.

Restaurant or liquor shop → none of these four

These trades need workflow software, not billing software: BillTouch POS for restaurants (KOT, tables, Zomato/Swiggy), BillTouch Liquor for wine shops (size-wise stock, excise registers).
