Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vyapar app free to use?

Yes, Vyapar has a free plan for mobile that includes basic GST billing, invoicing, and inventory management. Paid plans (Silver and Gold) unlock features like Tally import, SMS alerts, and multi-device access.

What are the Vyapar plan prices in 2026?

Vyapar has 6 paid plans (all + GST). Basic Plan: Silver at ₹3,399/yr (Desktop) and Gold at ₹3,699/yr (Desktop). Vertical Pro: Retail Pro, Distributor Pro, and Manufacturing Pro — each at ₹8,999/yr (Desktop). Premium: Platinum at ₹9,999/yr (Desktop). Add Mobile access for ₹600 extra (Silver/Gold) or ₹1,000–₹2,000 extra (Pro/Platinum). 3-year plans save up to 35%.

Can Vyapar be used offline?

Yes, Vyapar works fully offline on Android. All data is stored locally on your device and syncs when you reconnect to the internet.

Does Vyapar support GST filing?

Vyapar generates GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B reports automatically. You can export these reports and file them on the GST portal. It does not directly file returns, but makes the process very easy.

Is Vyapar better than Tally for small businesses?

For very small businesses and first-time software users, Vyapar is often better than Tally because it is mobile-first, easier to learn, and has a free plan. Tally is more suitable for businesses with complex accounting needs or chartered accountants.
