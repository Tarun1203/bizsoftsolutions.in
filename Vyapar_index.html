FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Is Vyapar app really free?

Yes — Vyapar offers a free plan with basic billing and inventory features. For unlimited invoices, Windows app, and advanced reports, you'll need the paid Silver or Gold plan starting around ₹1,499/year.

Does Vyapar work without internet?

Yes. This is one of Vyapar's biggest advantages over cloud-only software. It works fully offline on your phone or PC. Data syncs when you reconnect to the internet.

Is Vyapar better than Tally for small businesses?

For small businesses, Vyapar is often a better choice than Tally because it's far simpler to use, mobile-friendly, and much cheaper. Tally is better for large businesses needing advanced accounting. See our Vyapar vs Tally comparison for a detailed breakdown.

Can I use Vyapar on both phone and PC?

Yes. The paid plans include both Android and Windows apps. Your data syncs across all devices automatically.

Does Vyapar support GST filing?

Vyapar generates GSTR-1, GSTR-2, GSTR-3B reports in the correct format. You or your CA can use these to file returns. It doesn't directly file on the GST portal, but it makes the process very easy.

Is my data safe in Vyapar?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device and backed up securely. Vyapar does not share your business data with third parties.
