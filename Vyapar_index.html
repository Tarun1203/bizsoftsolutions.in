Common Questions
Vyapar FAQ
Everything you need to know before you start
What is Vyapar app?▼
Vyapar is India's most popular GST billing and inventory management app for small businesses. It lets you create invoices, track stock, manage accounts, and generate GST reports from your phone or computer. It's used by 1 crore+ businesses across India.
Is Vyapar free?▼
Vyapar offers a free trial so you can test the app before buying. Paid plans start at ₹3,399/year + GST (Silver, Desktop) and include GST billing, e-Invoice, e-Way Bill, inventory, and GSTR reports. The Desktop + Mobile combo (Silver) starts at ₹3,999/year + GST.
Does Vyapar work offline?▼
Yes, Vyapar works fully offline. You can create invoices, update stock, and record transactions without any internet connection. All data syncs automatically when your device connects to the internet. This is one of Vyapar's biggest advantages over cloud-only tools like Zoho Books.
What is the difference between Vyapar Silver and Gold?▼
Gold (offer price ₹4,323/yr, Desktop) is the most popular plan — it includes e-Invoice, unlimited e-Way Bills, inventory, GSTR reports, journal entries, OCR for bills, automated payment reminders, TCS/TDS, and up to 5 companies. Silver is the entry-level plan with fewer features. For most small businesses, Gold is the recommended choice. Contact us for current Silver pricing.
Does Vyapar support e-Invoice (IRN)?▼
Yes, Vyapar supports e-Invoice generation (IRN) from within the app. This is available in the Silver and Gold plans. You can generate e-Invoices for B2B transactions as required by GSTN, without needing to use a separate portal.
Can I use Vyapar on multiple devices?▼
Yes. The Silver plan allows 2 users and the Gold plan allows up to 5 users. Each user can access Vyapar on their own device. The owner can control access levels — for example, restrict certain staff from seeing profit reports.
Is Vyapar better than Tally Prime?▼
Vyapar is better for small businesses that want a simple, affordable, mobile-friendly tool they can operate themselves. Tally Prime is better for medium-to-large businesses with complex accounts, payroll, and a dedicated accountant. Vyapar Gold starts at ₹4,323/year (offer price); Tally Prime costs ₹18,000+ per year and requires accountant training. For most Indian SMBs, Vyapar is the right choice.
Can I import data from Tally to Vyapar?▼
Yes, the Gold plan includes a Tally Import/Export feature. You can migrate your existing Tally data (parties, items, opening balances) into Vyapar without re-entering everything manually. Contact us for migration assistance — we help with this as part of our free setup guidance.
Does Vyapar have a Windows / desktop version?▼
Yes! Vyapar is available for Android, iOS (Gold plan), and Windows desktop. The Windows version is great for shop owners who want to bill from a computer with a keyboard and larger screen, while also having the Android app for on-the-go access.
