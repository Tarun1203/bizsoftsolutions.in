What to Look For in Restaurant POS Software

  1. Zomato & Swiggy integration — essential if you get online orders
  2. Kitchen Display System (KDS) — reduces errors between front desk and kitchen
  3. Table management — visual floor plan for dine-in restaurants
  4. Offline mode — must work if internet goes down during service
  5. GST compliance — auto-generate GST invoices and reports
  6. Inventory tracking — monitor ingredient and item stock levels
  7. Staff management — shift tracking, roles, and permissions

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best POS software for restaurants in India?

Bill Touch is our top pick for Indian restaurants and liquor shops. It has Zomato/Swiggy integration, KDS, table management, and multi-outlet support. Petpooja is also popular for its ease of use.

Does restaurant POS software integrate with Zomato and Swiggy?

Yes, many Indian restaurant POS systems including Bill Touch, Petpooja, and Posist integrate directly with Zomato and Swiggy. Orders placed on these platforms flow automatically into the POS.

What is a KDS in restaurant POS?

KDS stands for Kitchen Display System. It's a screen in the kitchen that shows incoming orders from the POS. Chefs can see new orders and mark them as complete, improving communication between front-of-house and kitchen.
