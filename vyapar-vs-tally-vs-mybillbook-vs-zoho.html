Detailed Reviews

Vyapar — Best for Indian SMBs

Vyapar is India's most popular billing app for a reason. The free plan is genuinely useful, it works completely offline on Android, and the interface is designed for non-accountants. WhatsApp invoice sharing, thermal printer support, and GSTR auto-reports are all included.

Best for: Retail shops, kirana stores, pharmacies, service businesses, traders

My BillBook — Best Budget Option

My BillBook is a clean, simple billing app from the founders of IndiaMART. Its interface is very easy and the free plan covers basic invoicing. Slightly cheaper than Vyapar's paid plan. The inventory and reporting features are less powerful than Vyapar but sufficient for very small shops.

Best for: Very small shops, freelancers, first-time software users

Zoho Books — Best for Cloud-First Businesses

Zoho Books is a full-featured cloud accounting platform. It's great if you want client portals, online payment integration, and integration with other Zoho apps (CRM, Projects, etc.). However it requires an internet connection at all times — not ideal for areas with poor connectivity.

Best for: Service businesses, consultants, e-commerce sellers, tech-savvy owners

Tally Prime — Best for Complex Accounting

Tally remains the gold standard for Indian accounting professionals. Its features for manufacturing (Bill of Materials), payroll, cost centres, and multi-location inventory are unmatched. The steep price and learning curve mean it's overkill for most small businesses.

Best for: Medium-large businesses, manufacturing, businesses with a dedicated CA/accountant

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best billing software for small business in India?

For most Indian small businesses, Vyapar is the best billing software because it has a free plan, is easy to use, works offline, and is affordable. My BillBook is a close alternative. Tally is better for businesses with an accountant.

Is Zoho Books good for Indian small businesses?

Zoho Books is a good cloud-based option but starts at ₹2,999/year and requires internet. It is best for businesses that want cloud access, client portal, and integration with other Zoho apps. For offline and mobile use, Vyapar is stronger.

My BillBook vs Vyapar — which is better?

Both are good for small businesses. Vyapar has a stronger mobile offline experience and is more popular. My BillBook has a simpler interface and is slightly cheaper. The best choice depends on whether you need desktop access and offline mobile mode.
