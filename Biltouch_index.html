FAQ
Common Questions About Bill Touch
Does Bill Touch work on any Android device?

Yes. Bill Touch runs on any Android phone or tablet. No special hardware required — your existing Android device is enough to get started.

How does the Zomato and Swiggy integration work?

Once integrated, orders placed on Zomato and Swiggy automatically appear on your Bill Touch POS screen. You accept the order, it goes to the kitchen (KDS), and the bill is generated automatically — without any manual entry.

What is a Kitchen Display System (KDS)?

A KDS is a screen placed in the kitchen that shows orders in real time as they are placed at the counter or by captains. It replaces kitchen order tickets (KOTs) and reduces errors in order preparation.

Can I use Bill Touch for multiple branches?

Yes. Bill Touch supports multi-branch operations with a centralised dashboard to see all branches' performance from one place.

Is Bill Touch GST compliant?

Yes. Bill Touch generates fully GST-compliant receipts with CGST, SGST, and IGST calculations. You can print or send digital bills via WhatsApp.
