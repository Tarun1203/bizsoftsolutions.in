Common Questions
Bill Touch FAQ
Everything you need to know before getting started
What is Bill Touch?▼
Bill Touch is a comprehensive POS and billing software for Indian food and beverage businesses. It covers restaurants, cafes, cloud kitchens, bars, and liquor shops. Key features include Zomato/Swiggy integration, Kitchen Display System (KDS), table management, liquor inventory tracking, and GST billing — all on Android devices.
Does Bill Touch support Zomato and Swiggy integration?▼
Yes. Bill Touch integrates directly with Zomato and Swiggy. Orders placed by customers on these platforms appear automatically in your Bill Touch system and are sent straight to the Kitchen Display System. No manual relay needed — this eliminates missed orders and communication errors between front-of-house and kitchen.
What is KDS (Kitchen Display System) in Bill Touch?▼
KDS (Kitchen Display System) is a screen mounted in the kitchen that shows incoming orders in real-time. Instead of printed paper chits, kitchen staff see digital order tickets on the screen. They can acknowledge, prepare, and mark items as done. This speeds up kitchen operations, reduces errors, and eliminates lost/wet paper chits.
Does Bill Touch work for liquor shops?▼
Yes — and this is a major differentiator. Bill Touch has a dedicated Liquor Management module that includes bottle and nip-wise billing, excise-compliant invoice format, brand-wise inventory tracking, bar tab management, wastage recording, supplier purchase entries, and shift-wise closing reports. No other mainstream restaurant POS in India has built-in liquor management at this level.
Does Bill Touch work offline?▼
Yes. Bill Touch functions fully offline for billing, KDS, and order management. Data syncs when internet connection is restored. This is critical for restaurants in areas with unstable internet — your billing never stops even during outages.
What hardware is required for Bill Touch?▼
Bill Touch runs on any Android tablet or smartphone. For a typical restaurant setup, you'd need: 1 Android tablet for the billing counter (₹8,000–₹15,000), 1–2 phones for waiters (captain app), a 58mm or 80mm thermal printer (₹2,000–₹5,000), and optionally a second Android screen for KDS. No expensive proprietary POS hardware required.
Can I manage multiple restaurant outlets?▼
Yes. Bill Touch supports multi-outlet management. You can manage all your outlets from a single account, view consolidated sales reports, and set outlet-specific menus and pricing. This is ideal for restaurant chains, franchise owners, and cloud kitchen operators managing multiple brands.
Does Bill Touch support UPI payments?▼
Yes. Bill Touch integrates with UPI, credit/debit card terminals, and cash payments. You can also accept split payments — for example, part cash and part UPI for the same bill. Payment records are automatically captured in the billing system for accurate cash closing.
How is Bill Touch different from Petpooja?▼
Both are strong restaurant POS systems, but Bill Touch has two key advantages: (1) Built-in Liquor Management — Petpooja does not support liquor shop billing. (2) Offline reliability — multiple users report Bill Touch is more stable offline than Petpooja. Pricing is also competitive. If you run a bar, pub, or liquor shop alongside a restaurant, Bill Touch is the clear choice.
