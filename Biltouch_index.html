Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bill Touch POS software?

Bill Touch is a comprehensive Point of Sale (POS) software designed for restaurants, hotels, cloud kitchens, and liquor shops in India. It includes table management, kitchen display system, Zomato/Swiggy integration, and liquor shop billing features.

Does Bill Touch integrate with Zomato and Swiggy?

Yes, Bill Touch integrates directly with Zomato and Swiggy. Online orders from these platforms appear automatically in your POS system and are sent to the kitchen — no manual entry needed.

Can Bill Touch be used for a liquor shop?

Yes, Bill Touch has a dedicated module for liquor shops. It handles liquor-specific billing requirements, brand-wise stock tracking, excise compliance reports, and customer purchase history.

What is a Kitchen Display System (KDS) in Bill Touch?

A Kitchen Display System (KDS) in Bill Touch shows incoming orders on a screen in the kitchen. Chefs can see and mark orders as prepared, improving communication between the front and back of house.

Does Bill Touch support multiple restaurant outlets?

Yes, Bill Touch supports multi-outlet management. You can run multiple restaurant branches from a single dashboard, with consolidated reports, shared menus, and centralized stock management.
