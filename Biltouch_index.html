Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bill Touch?

Bill Touch is a POS billing software designed for restaurants, hotels, bars, cafes, and liquor shops in India. It supports table billing, KOT printing, liquor compliance billing, and daily sales reports.

Does Bill Touch support liquor shop billing?

Yes, Bill Touch has a dedicated liquor shop module with compliance billing features required for liquor retail businesses in India, including permit tracking and regulatory report generation.

Does Bill Touch work on Android tablets?

Yes, Bill Touch works on Android tablets and smartphones, making it easy to use at the counter or at the table for order taking.

Can Bill Touch print KOT (Kitchen Order Tickets)?

Yes, Bill Touch supports KOT printing on thermal printers. Orders are sent directly to the kitchen printer, reducing errors and speeding up service.

What reports does Bill Touch provide?

Bill Touch provides daily sales reports, item-wise sales, table-wise revenue, expense tracking, GST reports, and end-of-day settlement reports — everything a restaurant or bar owner needs.
