Best GST Billing Software in India 2026 — Top 7 Ranked

By BizSoft Solutions · Updated June 2026 · 8 min read

With GST compliance mandatory for all registered businesses in India, choosing the right billing software can save you hours every month. We've tested and ranked the top 7 GST billing software options available in India in 2026.

#1Vyapar
Starting at ₹3,399+GST/year · Android, Windows, iOS, macOS

India's most popular GST billing app for small businesses. Extremely easy to use, works offline, and covers billing, inventory, GST reports, and more. 6 plan options from Silver to Platinum.

Best for SMBsOfflineMobile
#2Tally Prime
Starting at ₹18,000+GST/year · Windows

The gold standard for Indian accounting software. Best for mid-to-large businesses and CA firms. Powerful but complex — not ideal for first-time users or small shops.

Best for CAsFull AccountingPayroll
#3My BillBook
Free plan available · Paid from ₹1,799/year · Android, Windows

Good free option for very small businesses just starting out. Limited inventory features but solid GST billing. Best for shops needing basic invoicing without spending money.

Free PlanSimpleMobile
#4Zoho Books
Free plan for businesses under ₹50L turnover · Paid from ₹2,999/year

Cloud-based accounting software great for service businesses, freelancers, and businesses needing client portals. GST-compliant with strong automation.

CloudService BizAutomation
#5Busy Accounting
Starting at ₹9,000+GST/year · Windows

Strong mid-range accounting software popular in North India. Good for distribution and manufacturing businesses needing more power than Vyapar but less complex than Tally.

DistributionManufacturingWindows
#6ClearTax GST
Free plan available · Cloud-based

Best known as a tax filing platform, ClearTax also offers GST billing and e-invoicing tools. Ideal for businesses that want filing and billing in one place. Limited inventory features.

E-InvoiceFilingCloud
#7My BillBook (Paid)
From ₹1,799/year · Android, Windows

The paid plans of My BillBook add more inventory features, multi-user support, and better reporting over the free version. A solid budget option for small shops that have outgrown the free plan but don't need Vyapar's full feature set.

BudgetSimpleMobile

Our Recommendation

For most Indian small businesses — kirana shops, wholesale, retail, or service businesses — Vyapar is the best GST billing software in 2026. It's affordable, easy to use, works offline, and covers everything from GST invoicing to inventory management and GSTR reports.

If you need full accounting power with payroll and complex reporting, go with Tally Prime. For a free starting point, My BillBook or Zoho Books (under ₹50L turnover) are solid choices.
