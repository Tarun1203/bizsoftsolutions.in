Biz Analyst vs CredFlow vs LiveKeeping 2026 — Best Tally Mobile App?

By BizSoft Solutions · Updated June 2026 · 8 min read

If you use Tally ERP 9 or TallyPrime and want to access your accounts, outstanding reports, and party ledgers on your smartphone — you have three main options: Biz Analyst, CredFlow, and LiveKeeping. All three sync with Tally and show data on mobile. But they serve different needs.

This article breaks down exactly how they compare so you can make the right choice for your business.

Quick Overview

🏆 Biz Analyst Our Pick
Starting at ₹3,300/year · Android & iOS · TallyPrime + Tally ERP 9 compatible

The most comprehensive Tally mobile companion. Gives you 100+ reports from Tally on your phone — from ledger outstandings to P&L, stock reports to sales analytics. Also lets you create orders, receipts, and share statements directly from the app. Best for business owners who want a full view of their Tally data on mobile.

✅ Pros
  • 100+ Tally reports on mobile
  • Create sales orders & receipts
  • WhatsApp statement sharing
  • Works offline (cached data)
  • Android & iOS
❌ Cons
  • Requires TCP connector setup
  • Tally PC must be on & connected
  • Learning curve for full features
📊 CredFlow
Freemium model · Paid plans from ₹2,499/year · Android & iOS

Focused specifically on receivables management — collecting money from outstanding debtors. Syncs with Tally to pull your debtor list and lets you send WhatsApp payment reminders, schedule follow-ups, and track collection progress. Not a full Tally viewer, but excellent if your pain point is unpaid invoices.

✅ Pros
  • Best-in-class debtor follow-up
  • WhatsApp payment reminders
  • Automated collection workflows
  • Free plan available
❌ Cons
  • Not a full Tally viewer
  • Limited to receivables focus
  • No inventory/stock reports
📱 LiveKeeping
Freemium · Paid from ₹1,499/year · Android

A lighter Tally mobile companion focused on quick access to party ledgers, sales/purchase summaries, and daily transactions. Good for smaller businesses that want a simple, affordable way to check Tally data from a phone. Android only; fewer reports than Biz Analyst.

✅ Pros
  • Very affordable
  • Simple interface — easy to learn
  • Quick ledger lookup
  • Free plan available
❌ Cons
  • Android only (no iOS)
  • Fewer reports than Biz Analyst
  • No order creation
  • Smaller company & support

Which One Should You Choose?

Choose Biz Analyst if you want complete access to your Tally data from your phone — reports, ledgers, stock, P&L, orders. It's the most capable option and worth every rupee for business owners who travel or need to check accounts on the go.

Choose CredFlow if your main headache is collecting money from debtors. Its automated WhatsApp reminder workflows and collection pipeline are unmatched. Great as a supplement to Biz Analyst, or as a standalone tool if receivables is all you need.

Choose LiveKeeping if you're on a very tight budget and need simple Android-only access to Tally ledgers. It's a basic but functional tool — good for checking party outstanding and daily transactions.

Bottom Line For most Tally users who want a rich mobile experience, Biz Analyst is the clear winner with 100+ reports, order creation, and both Android & iOS support. If you specifically want to automate debtor collections, add CredFlow. LiveKeeping is for those with minimal needs and a limited budget.
