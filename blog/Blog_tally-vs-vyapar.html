Tally vs Vyapar 2026 — Which is Better for Indian Small Businesses?

By BizSoft Solutions · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read

Choosing between Tally and Vyapar is one of the most common decisions Indian small business owners face. Both are popular, GST-compliant, and made for Indian businesses — but they serve very different needs.

In this article, we compare Tally Prime and Vyapar across pricing, ease of use, features, and the type of business each is best for.

Pricing — Vyapar Wins for Small Businesses

Tally Prime starts at around ₹18,000+GST per year for a single user license. For a small kirana shop or service business, that's a significant investment — especially when you're just starting out.

Vyapar's Silver plan starts at ₹3,399+GST/year, and even the most feature-rich Platinum plan is ₹9,999+GST/year — still well below Tally's entry price. For price-sensitive small businesses, Vyapar is the clear winner.

Ease of Use — Vyapar is Far Simpler

Tally was designed for accountants. The interface uses function keys, requires accounting knowledge, and has a steep learning curve. Most small business owners need weeks of training to use Tally independently.

Vyapar, on the other hand, was built for business owners — not accountants. You can create your first GST invoice within 5 minutes of installing the app. No accounting knowledge required.

Features — Tally is More Powerful

For complex accounting needs — payroll, manufacturing (detailed), multi-currency, custom reports, statutory compliance — Tally Prime is significantly more powerful than Vyapar. If you work with a chartered accountant and need full double-entry bookkeeping with audit trails, Tally is the better choice.

Vyapar covers all the basics very well — GST billing, inventory, payments, GSTR reports — and the Pro plans add manufacturing, distribution, and retail-specific features.

Mobile Access — Vyapar Wins

Vyapar's mobile app is a first-class experience. You can create invoices, check stock, view reports, and manage your business entirely from your Android phone — even offline. The Biz Analyst app can give Tally data on mobile, but it's a separate purchase.

Our Verdict Choose Vyapar if you're a small business owner who wants simple, affordable GST billing and inventory management without needing an accountant.

Choose Tally Prime if you have a chartered accountant, need payroll, have complex accounting requirements, or run a mid-to-large business.
