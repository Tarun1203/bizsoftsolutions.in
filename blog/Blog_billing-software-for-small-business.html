Best Billing Software for Small Business in India 2026 — Matched by Business Type

By BizSoft Solutions · Updated June 2026 · 6 min read

Not every business has the same billing needs. A kirana shop needs fast counter billing; a distributor needs route tracking; a restaurant needs KOT. We've matched the best billing software to each Indian business type so you don't have to guess.

Choose Your Business Type

🛒
Kirana Shop / General Store
✅ Recommended: Vyapar Gold or Retail Pro

Vyapar is perfect for kirana shops — fast billing, barcode scanning, inventory tracking, and WhatsApp invoice sharing. The Retail Pro plan adds POS and loyalty points for growing shops.

🏭
Wholesale / Distribution Business
✅ Recommended: Vyapar Distributor Pro

The Distributor Pro plan includes field salesman tracking, party-wise item rates, godown management, and bulk WhatsApp messaging — everything a wholesale or distribution business needs.

⚙️
Manufacturing Business
✅ Recommended: Vyapar Manufacturing Pro or Tally Prime

For small manufacturers, Vyapar Manufacturing Pro covers job work challans, manufacturing reports, and consumption tracking. For complex multi-stage manufacturing with full costing, Tally Prime is better.

💊
Pharmacy / Medical Store
✅ Recommended: Marg ERP or Vyapar Gold

Pharmacies need expiry date tracking, batch management, and drug schedule compliance. Marg ERP is built specifically for pharmacies. Vyapar Gold works for simpler pharmacy billing needs.

🍽️
Restaurant / Cafe / Bar
✅ Recommended: Bill Touch

Bill Touch is built for Indian food and beverage businesses — table billing, KOT printing, GST invoices, liquor compliance, and daily settlement reports. Fast, affordable for single-outlet setups.

🍾
Liquor Shop / Wine Store
✅ Recommended: Bill Touch

Bill Touch has a dedicated liquor compliance module with excise-ready reporting, SKU-level tracking, and daily settlement — built specifically for Indian liquor shops and wine outlets.

🔧
Service Business (Electrician, Plumber, Salon, Repair)
✅ Recommended: Vyapar Silver or Zoho Books

Service businesses need quotation, invoice, and payment tracking more than inventory. Vyapar Silver handles this well offline. Zoho Books is better if you send a lot of client-facing documents and want automated reminders.

📊
Existing Tally Users Who Want Mobile Access
✅ Recommended: Biz Analyst

If you already use Tally ERP 9 or TallyPrime and want to check reports, outstanding, and party ledgers on your phone — Biz Analyst is the best Tally mobile companion app in India.
