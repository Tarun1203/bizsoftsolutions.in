Running a restaurant in India in 2026 means managing dine-in tables, online orders from Zomato and Swiggy, a kitchen display system, billing, and GST — often all at the same time. The right restaurant POS software can make this manageable. The wrong one turns it into chaos.

We've compared the top restaurant billing and POS systems available in India to help you make the right choice for your business.

Quick Answer: For small to mid-size restaurants and cloud kitchens that need Zomato + Swiggy integration without expensive hardware, Bill Touch is one of the best options — works on any Android device and handles all order channels from one screen.

What to Look for in Restaurant POS Software

  • Online order integration — Zomato and Swiggy orders should auto-appear in the POS
  • Kitchen Display System (KDS) — orders go to kitchen screen without paper tickets
  • Table management — visual floor plan, table status, easy order management
  • GST compliance — correct tax calculation on food, beverages, and services
  • Offline capability — should work even if internet drops briefly
  • Hardware requirements — does it need expensive dedicated hardware or works on Android?
  • Pricing — monthly cost vs. value, especially for small restaurants

2. Petpooja — Most Widely Used Restaurant POS in India

Petpooja

Rating: ⭐ 4.4/5 Pricing: ~₹10,000–₹15,000/year Best for: Mid-size restaurants, chains

Petpooja is one of the most established restaurant POS systems in India, used by 70,000+ restaurants. It has a comprehensive feature set — billing, KDS, table management, Zomato/Swiggy integration, inventory, and CRM.

✅ Pros

  • Very mature, proven software
  • Strong integrations ecosystem
  • Good support network
  • Zomato/Swiggy integrated

❌ Cons

  • More expensive than Bill Touch
  • Can be complex for small outlets
  • Setup takes time

3. POSist — Best for Restaurant Chains & Fine Dining

POSist

Rating: ⭐ 4.6/5 Pricing: Premium pricing (custom quotes) Best for: Restaurant chains, fine dining, QSR chains

POSist is a cloud-based enterprise restaurant management platform used by major chains in India and internationally. It's powerful but priced for chains — not ideal for a single restaurant.

✅ Pros

  • Enterprise-grade features
  • Multi-outlet management
  • Advanced analytics
  • International presence

❌ Cons

  • Too expensive for small restaurants
  • Overkill for single outlets
  • Complex to set up

4. Torqus — Good All-round Option

Torqus POS

Rating: ⭐ 4.2/5 Pricing: ~₹8,000–₹12,000/year Best for: Mid-size restaurants, cafes

Torqus (now owned by Swiggy) is a solid restaurant POS with good features. Its acquisition by Swiggy means the Swiggy integration is particularly strong.

✅ Pros

  • Strong Swiggy integration
  • Good feature set
  • Reasonable pricing

❌ Cons

  • Less independent since Swiggy acquisition
  • Support quality inconsistent
