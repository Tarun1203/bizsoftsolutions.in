Which Restaurant POS Should You Choose?

For most Indian restaurants, hotels, bars, and liquor shops — Bill Touch is the best starting point. It's built specifically for the Indian F&B market, handles table billing and KOT out of the box, and is far more affordable than Petpooja or Posist.
