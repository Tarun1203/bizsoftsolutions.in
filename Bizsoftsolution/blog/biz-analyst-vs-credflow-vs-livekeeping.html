1. What Each App Actually Does

📊 Biz Analyst

Biz Analyst is a sales intelligence and team management app that syncs with your Tally ERP/Prime. Its primary use case is giving business owners and sales managers a real-time view of sales performance, outstanding payments, and field team activity — all from their phone. It's built for distribution businesses, FMCG companies, and any business with a field sales team.

Key differentiator: Live GPS tracking of sales reps, beat plan management, party-wise outstanding with automated WhatsApp reminders, and order booking on-the-go.

💰 Credflow

Credflow is a working capital and receivables management platform. While it integrates with Tally (and also ERP systems), its focus is on automating collections — sending payment reminders, tracking overdue invoices, and helping businesses reduce Days Sales Outstanding (DSO). It's more of a fintech tool than a pure Tally companion.

Key differentiator: Automated multi-channel payment reminders (WhatsApp, SMS, email), invoice discounting, early payment programs, and credit analytics.

📱 LiveKeeping

LiveKeeping is a lightweight Tally data viewer. It connects to your Tally server and lets you view reports, ledger balances, party statements, and stock levels from your phone — but with limited action capabilities. It's the simplest of the three.

Key differentiator: Easiest to set up, lowest cost, best for owners who just want to check Tally reports without going to the office.

4. Receivables & Collections — Credflow Specialises Here

For businesses with large outstanding receivables — distributors, manufacturers, service companies — Credflow offers the most automated collections workflow.

  • Auto payment reminders via WhatsApp, SMS, and email at configurable intervals (7 days, 15 days, 30 days overdue)
  • Invoice-level tracking — know exactly which invoice is overdue, by how much, and for how long
  • Credit scoring of customers based on payment history
  • Invoice discounting — get early payment on outstanding invoices through Credflow's NBFC network
  • DSO dashboard — track Days Sales Outstanding over time
  • Payment links — send customers a direct payment link via WhatsApp

💡 When to use Credflow: If your business has ₹20 lakh+ in outstanding receivables and you're spending hours manually chasing payments, Credflow's automation can dramatically cut your collection time. It works best alongside Biz Analyst — use Biz Analyst for sales operations and Credflow for collections.

7. Who Should Use Which?

✅ Choose Biz Analyst if…

Biz Analyst

You have a field sales team of 3+ reps. You're a distributor, FMCG brand, or manufacturer. You want GPS tracking, beat planning, order booking on mobile, and party-wise sales reports — all synced live with Tally.

✅ Choose Credflow if…

Credflow

You have large outstanding receivables (₹20L+) and want to automate collections. You want automated WhatsApp/SMS payment reminders sent to customers without manual effort. You're interested in invoice discounting for working capital.

✅ Choose LiveKeeping if…

LiveKeeping

You're a business owner who just wants to check Tally reports — sales, stock, party ledger — from your phone, without the cost or complexity of a full sales management platform. Smallest business or solo operation.

💡 Pro tip: Biz Analyst and Credflow are complementary, not competing. Many distribution businesses use Biz Analyst for sales operations + order booking, and Credflow for automated collections. Together they cover the full order-to-cash cycle on top of Tally.
