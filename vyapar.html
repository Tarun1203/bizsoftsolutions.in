FAQs

Vyapar — Common Questions

What is the difference between Vyapar Silver and Gold?
Silver covers a single device with all core billing, inventory and GST features. Gold adds multi-user sync (desktop + mobile together), user roles, and premium features like online store and priority support. Most shops with one counter start with Silver; businesses where the owner monitors remotely choose Gold.
Is Vyapar a one-time purchase or subscription?
Vyapar is a yearly subscription. Multi-year (3-year) packs bring the effective yearly cost down significantly — we'll quote you the current multi-year offer.
Does Vyapar work without internet?
Yes. Vyapar is offline-first: billing, stock and reports work fully offline. Internet is needed only for backup, sync between devices, and WhatsApp sharing.
Can I import my old data into Vyapar?
Yes — items and parties can be imported from Excel, and we do this for you free during setup. Opening stock and outstanding balances are also set up so you start with correct books.
Is my data safe? Where is it stored?
Your data lives on your own device with encrypted automatic backups to your Google Drive. Even Vyapar (the company) can't read your business data.
Does Vyapar file GST returns?
Vyapar prepares GSTR-1, GSTR-3B and other GST report data ready for filing. You or your CA file on the GST portal — the export takes minutes instead of days of manual compilation.
