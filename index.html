Built specifically for Indian SMBs, Vyapar supports GST, TDS, e-Invoice (IRN), e-Way Bill, and GSTR exports. No accountant required — the interface is in simple Hindi and English.

Bill Touch

Complete POS & Billing for Restaurants, Cafes & Food Businesses

Bill Touch is a full-featured restaurant POS and billing system designed for Indian F&B businesses. It handles dine-in table management, takeaway, delivery via Zomato & Swiggy, and even KDS (Kitchen Display System) on a single platform.

Works on any Android tablet or phone — no expensive POS hardware needed. Thermal printer support, real-time reports, multi-outlet management, and UPI/card payment integration included.
