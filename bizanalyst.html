FAQs

Biz Analyst — Common Questions

Does Biz Analyst change or risk my Tally data?
No. It reads your Tally data through a secure sync utility. Vouchers created from mobile arrive in Tally as normal entries your accountant can verify. Your Tally remains the single source of truth.
Does my Tally computer need to stay on?
The sync app on the Tally computer pushes data when it's on. Once synced, your mobile shows the latest snapshot even when the office PC is off — it updates again when the PC comes back online.
Which Tally versions are supported?
Tally ERP 9 and TallyPrime, both Silver and Gold licenses, single or multi-company. We confirm compatibility during the free demo.
How is it priced — per company or per user?
Per user per year, with all your Tally companies included. Owner + accountant + 2 salespeople = 4 users. Multi-user packs get better rates from us.
Can my salesperson see my profits?
Only if you allow it. Role-based permissions let you hide margins, restrict parties, and limit each user to exactly what their job needs.
