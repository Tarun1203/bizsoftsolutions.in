Our take

Choose Biz Analyst if…

You want one app that covers everything — dashboards, reminders, mobile data entry AND a sales team using it in the field with controlled access. It's the most complete package, which is why it's our default recommendation. See features & live pricing.

Choose CredFlow if…

Your single biggest problem is stuck receivables and you want a dedicated collections engine (auto-escalating reminders, cashflow scoring). You give up mobile data entry and team features for it.

Choose LiveKeeping if…

You're a solo owner who mainly wants to see Tally data and occasionally punch a voucher, at the lowest price, with a simpler interface.

Whichever you pick: it reads your Tally — your books stay in Tally, your CA workflow doesn't change. The risk of trying one is low; all three offer trials.
