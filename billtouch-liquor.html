FAQs

Liquor Management — Common Questions

Does it match my state's excise register formats?
The registers cover the standard daily sale/stock/purchase formats and are configurable; during setup we map them to your state's requirements. Tell us your state in the enquiry and we'll confirm in the demo.
I have 2,000+ brand-size combinations. How long is setup?
We import the brand master from Excel (or our ready master list for your state) and set opening stock with you. Typical go-live is 1–2 days including barcode mapping and staff training.
Can my staff bill without seeing purchase rates or profits?
Yes. Counter login only bills and views stock; margins, purchase rates and reports are owner/manager-only with PIN or password.
What if a bottle has no barcode or the barcode won't scan?
Quick-search by brand name or shortcut code works alongside scanning. You can also print your own barcode labels from the software for loose or unlabeled stock.
Does it work offline?
Yes, fully — billing, stock and registers run on your counter PC without internet. Cloud backup and the owner's phone reports sync when internet is available.
Can it handle both my wine shop and attached permit room?
Yes — shop sales (bottle) and permit-room sales (peg-wise) run side by side with correct stock deduction and separate reporting for each.
